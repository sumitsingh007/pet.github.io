Overview

Digitization is continuously changing how business firms adopt technologies, build strategies and conduct operations. At petonic—we help clients to drive changes through excellent financial services using innovation, advanced analytical tools, and accurate strategies which drive sustainable growth for the organization and customers. We help financial organizations make transformative moves that prepare them for unprecedented challenges.

How do we help our clients?

Insurance

We partner with global insurers to help them implement new solutions and use digital tools to unlock the new potentials of growth. Our experts provide a detailed analysis of how to capitalize on growth in this sector.

Law firms and Professional Services

We help leading global law firms to drive growth and profitability through a global strategy based on insights built through digital tools, the updated business models, and a rejuvenated approach to talent. We advise law firms and Professional Services on a broad range of organizational, operational, strategic, and functional issues.

Retail and Small Business Banking

We help a wide range of clients in banking to grow and find appropriate solutions in a dynamically changing environment. We help them to build the capabilities that they need for sustainable growth in the sector.

Payments

We help our clients to capture the right opportunities and evolve according to the shifting customer behavior and technology disruption. Our work and services range from processing, outsourcing to infrastructure acquiring, and all payment-related services and operations.

Wealth and Asset Management

Our unique services help companies develop capabilities that enable a more flexible, data-driven management of wealth and assets.

Marketing and Sales

We help business leaders transform their technologies and work functions to unlock efficiencies that provide more profitable growth. We help the market segments establish more customer-relevant experiences to drive their sales. In this goal-oriented world, what's needed to attract customers is a new model which incorporates the union of marketing and sales.

Operations and Technology

We understand that value in operations is crucial for building sustainable growth and gaining a competitive advantage. We help clients gain value from every aspect of their operations using appropriate tools and technologies. Our experts partner with clients to drive visible transformational changes and create an impact in the industry. We use innovative technologies to propel advancement, flexibility, and security, much needed for smooth financial operations in this digitalized world.

Organization

We work with intelligent approaches, dynamic organizational models, and profitable tools that transform the industry. We remodel techniques and provide better results by optimizing the organizational processing. Delivering a secure and scalable platform for financial services to our clients, we assure to fulfill their objectives.

Risk

We help brands to raise profits at lower costs, mitigate risks and liquidity problems, and gain a competitive advantage, making it easier to retain clients.

Corporate Strategy and Finance

We deliver clients' accurate strategies and precise initiatives. We analyze potential risks and prioritize core areas while working collaboratively with our clients. Our financial and strategy experts redesign techniques, automate operations and develop new capabilities to gain agility and productivity crucial for transformation.

At Petonic, our experts tackle difficult financial issues by combining their world-class skills, experience, and advanced technologies to deliver results that create impact and build trust.
